STEP 2 — DIAGNOSE (10 min)

Ask these in order. Listen more than you talk.

1. "What's your current role? How long you been in IT?"

2. "What got you interested in cybersecurity?"

3. "What's been holding you back from making the move?"

4. "If you made the switch — what would that mean for you financially? For your family?"

5. "On a scale of 1-10, how serious are you about making this happen in the next 3-6 months?"
