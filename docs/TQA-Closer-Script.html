Closer Call Script — Strategy Session

30-minute framework · DJ does NOT take these calls · The closer qualifies, diagnoses, and either enrolls or escalates to DJ welcome call for VIPs only.

Pre-Call Prep (2 Minutes)
Before every call, review the applicant's registration form answers and application form answers. Know their current role, how long they've been considering the move, and their qualification score before you pick up the phone.

1 Warm Open 2-3 minutes

Goal: Build rapport, set the frame, get permission to lead.

Closer
Hey [NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] from DJ's team at The Quantum Achievers. Thanks for taking the time. I know you're busy. Before we jump in, I want to be upfront: this isn't a sales call. My job is to understand where you are, where you want to go, and whether our program is genuinely the right fit. If it's not, I'll tell you. Sound fair?
Closer
Perfect. I've looked at your application so I have some context, but I'd love to hear it from you. What made you register for the webinar in the first place?
LET THEM TALK. Don't interrupt. Take notes on specific language they use. You'll mirror it back later.

2 Diagnosis 8-10 minutes

Goal: Uncover the real pain, timeline, and commitment level. Ask in natural conversation, not rapid-fire.

Current Situation

Closer
Walk me through your current role. What does a typical day look like for you right now?
Closer
How long have you been in this position?
Closer
On a scale of 1-10, how fulfilled are you in this role? [If low:] What's the biggest thing that's missing?

The Gap

Closer
When did you first start thinking about cybersecurity as a career path?
Closer
What have you tried so far? Courses, certifications, applications?
Closer
What's gotten in the way of making the move? [Listen for: time, money, confidence, direction, family pressure, visa concerns]

The Cost of Inaction

Closer
If nothing changes in the next 12 months. Same role, same salary, same ceiling. How does that feel?
Closer
What's this costing you right now? Not just financially, but in terms of how you feel about your career?
This is where the emotional weight hits. Give them space. Don't rush past this.

3 Program Fit 5-7 minutes

Goal: Present the program as the bridge between where they are and where they want to be. Use THEIR language from Phase 2.

Closer
Based on what you've told me — [mirror their exact situation back] — here's what the program would look like for someone in your position...

4 Objection Handling 5-8 minutes

The Big 3 objections for this avatar: Price, Time, and "Will This Work For Me."

"I need to think about it"

Totally understand. Can I ask — what specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the timing, or whether the program is the right fit for your situation? [Let them name it — then address THAT specific concern.]

"It's too expensive"

I hear you. Let me put it in context: you mentioned you're at [their current salary]. The average cybersecurity salary for someone with your background and 1-2 certifications is $110-130K. Even if the program only accelerates your timeline by 6 months, that's $25-50K in additional income you'd be earning sooner. The $4,999 investment pays for itself before you finish the program.

"I don't have time"

That's actually one of the most common things people say. And it usually means the current role is already taking everything you've got. The program is designed around working professionals. Most participants study 5-7 hours per week. The question isn't whether you have time. It's whether you can afford to keep spending ALL your time on a career that isn't taking you where you want to go.

"Will this work for someone with my background?"

That's exactly why the application exists. We don't accept everyone. We accepted you because your background IS the right foundation. DJ has mentored 250+ people, many with backgrounds very similar to yours. [Reference a specific success story that matches their situation.]

"I need to talk to my spouse/family"

Completely respect that. This is a family decision. Would it help if I sent you a one-page summary you can share with them? And let's schedule a follow-up call for [2-3 days out]. That way you have time to discuss it and I can answer any questions they might have too.

"What about my visa situation?"

That's exactly why having a structured, accelerated path matters more for you than for anyone else. You can't afford to "figure it out as you go" when your visa depends on your employment. The program gives you a clear, documented path to a new career — which actually strengthens your position, not weakens it.

VIP Escalation
If the lead is high-value (score 11-12, strong Q9 answer, high income bracket) AND expresses wanting to hear from DJ directly, schedule a 10-minute DJ welcome call. DJ's job on that call is ONLY to welcome and confirm. NOT to close. The closer has already done the work.
